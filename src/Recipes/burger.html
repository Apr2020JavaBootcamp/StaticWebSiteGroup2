Welcome to a Simple Hamburger Recipe!


Recipe Guide On How-To Make Your Own Hamburger

Nutrition Info
  • Prepare Time: 15-20 Minutes
  • Total Time: 35 Minutes

Directions:
Step 1: Preheat the pan or a cooking pan.
Step 2: Add Oil into the pan. (1-2minutes)
Step 3: Then add a whisk of Egg, a teaspoon of salt and pepper. (Cook for 2-3minutes, flip occasionally)
Step 4: After Egg is cooked until well done.
Step 5: Take the Egg and place it into a plate.
Step 6: Repeat the process of adding oil into the pan. (heat oil for 1-2 minutes)
Step 6: Add the ground beef into the cooking pan, cook it for 6-8 minutes on both sides. (Cook beef on both sides, flip occasionally 6-8 minutes each side).
Step 7: After the meat is done, place it on a plate.
Step 8: Then heat the pan and repeat the process for Step 1-2.
Step 9: Place Bun on the pan, cook it for 1-2 minutes.
Step 10: Put Ground Beef on the Bun, add optional ingredients of Lettuce, Tomato.

List of Ingredients
- 1 Egg
- 1 Ground Beef
- Teaspoon of Salt
- Teaspoon of Black Pepper
- 1/2 Cup of Breadcrumbs
- Lettuce (Optional)
- Tomato (Optional)
- Ketchup (Optional)

These are the list of ingredients that will be needed in order to start the process of making an basic hamburger
